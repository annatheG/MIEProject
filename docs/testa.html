Welcome to the Memory Experiment

Hi everyone! Thank you for participating in our study about the effects of short-term memory recall. This task will take approximately 8-10 minutes to complete. Please read the following instructions carefully before starting.

Overview

In this study, you will be asked to memorize a list of words and then recall them from memory.

General Guidelines

  • Complete this task in a quiet environment without distractions.
  • Complete this task individually.
  • Do not write down, take pictures, or use external aids.
  • Use a computer or laptop.

Trial Procedure

Memorization Phase

Once you click "Proceed", a list of 15 randomly generated words will appear on the screen. You will have 30 seconds to study and memorize the list. After seconds, the list will automatically disappear, and you will be taken to the recall phase. You may also click "Proceed to Recall" to move on earlier.

Recall Phase

Type as many words as you can remember in any order. Submit words by pressing "Enter". Correct entries appear in their original positions. Incorrect words will not appear. Once all words are correctly recalled, your answers will be submitted automatically.
