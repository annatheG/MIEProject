Welcome to the Memory Experiment

Hi everyone! Thank you for participating in our study about the effects of short-term memory recall. This task will take approximately 5-8 minutes to complete. Please read the following instructions carefully before starting.

Overview

In this study, you will be asked to memorize a list of words and then recall them from memory. Your performance is based on the number of words you recall correctly and how quickly you recall them. These two metrics are weighted equally.

General Guidelines

  • Please complete this task in a quiet environment without distractions.
  • This task should be completed individually.
  • Do not write down, take pictures of the screen, or use any external aids.
  • Please complete this task on a computer or laptop.

Trial Procedure

Memorization Phase

Once you click "Start", a list of 15 words will appear on the screen. You will have 30 seconds to memorize the list. If you feel ready before the time is up, you may click "Proceed to recall phase". This will not change your score.

Recall Phase

After the list disappears, you will begin recalling the words. Type as many words as you can remember into the designated box, the words do not have to be in their original order. Please separate each word using the "enter" or "space" key on your keyboard. Do not use commas or other characters. The timer will begin at the start of this phase and stop when you click "Submit".

Review Phase

After submitting your responses, the words you recalled correctly will appear in their original positions in the list. Words that were not part of the list will be listed in red. You will have 10 seconds to review your results before proceeding to the next trial.
